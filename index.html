About Me

From the factory floor to the boardroom dashboard — a five-year arc spent making learning measurable.

Ashwini's path into Learning & Development began on the shop floor — coordinating training compliance across four manufacturing plants at Varroc Engineering, where audit-ready documentation and apprentice development were non-negotiable. That operational discipline became the foundation for everything since.

At ALTEN India, the remit expanded into full-cycle ownership: building the annual L&D strategy from organisation-wide needs analysis, coordinating 550+ training programmes, and designing the employee engagement calendar that lifted post-training performance by 70%.

Today, as Technology Learning Specialist at HSBC, Ashwini is the single point of contact for technical learning across 35+ countries — administering 8+ platforms and translating it all into dashboards business leaders actually act on.
